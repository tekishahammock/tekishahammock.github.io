About Tekisha

A blossoming web developer from Nashville, TN, I am currently attending Nashville Software School to learn necessary skills and technologies to become a junior developer. I am capable of handling full-stack web developement, designing stylish, user-friendly interfaces utilizing AngularJS, Sass and CSS Animations, followed by working with Python and Django to create back-end structure. I am known for writing clean, well-structured, well-planned and commented code that is easily understood by my teammates and instructors. I make a point of refactoring each project so that my code is as DRY as possible, while still being especially human readable. Prior to learning how to create software, I was a departmental subject matter expert, beta-tester, and test writer for several new software products during my time at WEX Fleet One.

In addition to my technological achievements, I have a background in leadership and customer service through my time at WEX Fleet One, T-Mobile, and the Walt Disney Company. I have frequently been recognized for my ability to communicate effectively with clients, resolve client concerns, and maintain solid relationships via phone, web, and face-to-face contact.
